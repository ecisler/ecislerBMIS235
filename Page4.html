Golf is Hard!

Since golf takes most up almost 25% of the day, why would anyone play?
The answer is simple. Golf is fun...until it isn't.
Golf is a true roller coaster. You can hit one good shot and be on top of the world.
Your next shot could end up in the woods, in the lake, or three feet in front of you.
That's when you feel like throwing your clubs in the water and never playing this game again.
